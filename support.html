Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't Body Battery syncing with my Apple Watch?
Make sure your iPhone and Apple Watch are both connected and Bluetooth is enabled. Open the Watch app on your iPhone, navigate to My Watch → Body Battery, and confirm the app is installed and set to "Show App on Apple Watch." A restart of both devices usually resolves sync issues.
Which Apple Watch models are supported?
Body Battery requires Apple Watch Series 4 or later running watchOS 9 or newer, paired with an iPhone running iOS 16 or later.
My data doesn't look accurate — what should I check?
Body Battery reads from Apple Health. Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Body Battery on your iPhone and make sure all data categories are enabled. Wearing your watch snugly during activity will also improve sensor accuracy.
How do I report a bug or crash?
Please email us at support@bodybattery.app with a short description of what happened and, if possible, the steps to reproduce it. Including your iPhone model and iOS version is very helpful.
How do I request a refund?
Purchases are processed by Apple. You can request a refund directly through Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Quick Troubleshooting Steps

1
Force-quit and reopen the app on your Apple Watch by pressing the side button, swiping the app card away, then reopening it.
2
Check Health permissions — Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Body Battery — and ensure all categories are allowed.
3
Restart both devices. Hold the side button on your Apple Watch and power off your iPhone, then turn them back on.
4
Reinstall the app by deleting it from your Watch and reinstalling via the Watch app on your iPhone.
5
Still stuck? Email us and we'll help you sort it out.
