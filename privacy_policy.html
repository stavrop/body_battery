🔒 1. Overview

Reservoir ("the App", "we", "our") is a health and wellness application for iPhone and Apple Watch. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and the protections in place to keep your information private.

The short version: Reservoir reads health data from Apple Health on your device, processes it entirely on-device to compute your energy score, and never transmits your health data to any external server, cloud service, or third party. Ever.

📊 2. Data We Access

Reservoir requests read-only access to the following Apple HealthKit data types. All data is accessed and processed exclusively on your device.

Data Type Why We Use It Stored?
Heart Rate Variability (SDNN) Primary recovery signal, normalised to your personal baseline On-device only
Resting Heart Rate Recovery indicator and part of your personal baseline On-device only
Heart Rate Drives real-time activity and stress drain, and workout intensity On-device only
Sleep Analysis & Stages Sleep is the primary refill window for your reservoir On-device only
Workout Sessions Used to compute training load (Banister TRIMP) and residual fatigue On-device only

You can grant or revoke any of these permissions at any time via Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Reservoir on your iPhone.

🚫 3. Data We Never Collect

Reservoir does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, or any other personal identifiers
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Any health data to external servers or the cloud
  • Device analytics, crash logs, or usage telemetry
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • Any data from your contacts, camera, microphone, or photos

No account required. Reservoir works without registration. We have no user accounts and no user database.

⚙️ 4. How We Use Your Data

All data processing happens locally on your iPhone and Apple Watch. Specifically, the App:

  • Reads health samples from HealthKit and replays them through an on-device energy model to compute your Reservoir score (0–100)
  • Stores a rolling 30-day history of your scores in local app storage to power trend graphs and personalised baselines
  • Caches your most recent score in a widget extension so it can be displayed on your Home Screen and Lock Screen without opening the App
  • Sends your current score to the Apple Watch companion app over local device communication (never the internet)
  • On supported devices, optionally generates written insights using Apple Intelligence. This runs entirely on-device — your health values are used only in the on-device prompt and are never sent to Apple or any server.

None of this data ever leaves your device.

🤝 5. Third Parties

Reservoir does not integrate any third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or data collection.

The App uses Apple HealthKit (provided by Apple Inc.) to read health data. Your use of HealthKit is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. Reservoir does not share any HealthKit data with Apple beyond what Apple already has through HealthKit itself.

There are no advertisements in Reservoir and we do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or any other commercial third parties.

👶 6. Children's Privacy

Reservoir is rated 4+ on the App Store. The App does not knowingly collect any personal data from children under the age of 13. Because Reservoir does not collect any personal data from anyone, it is safe for use by users of all ages under parental supervision.

⚖️ 7. Your Rights

Because Reservoir does not collect or store personal data on any server, there is no data to request, export, or delete from our side. However:

  • Revoke HealthKit access: Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Reservoir and toggle off any data types at any time.
  • Delete local app data: Deleting the Reservoir app from your iPhone removes all locally stored scores and history from your device.
  • Apple Health data: Your underlying health data is managed through the Apple Health app. Reservoir has read-only access and cannot modify or delete your health records.

If you are a resident of the European Union (GDPR), California (CCPA), or another jurisdiction with specific privacy rights, those rights are fully satisfied by the above — since we hold no personal data about you.
